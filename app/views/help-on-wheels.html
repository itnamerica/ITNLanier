Help on Wheels

As a proud affiliate of ITNAmerica, ITN{{affiliate}} provides dignified transportation for seniors, and adults with vision challenges.

    We help:
  • families stay connected
  • adult children minimize absenteeism from work
  • businesses remain profitable
  • seniors to begin a new chapter in their lives and remain independent

ITN{{affiliate}} provides over 2,000 rides a year in Hall County, Georgia. Our newest fundraising campaign, Help On Wheels, aims to cover the cost of these rides, and will allow ITN to continue to grow and serve the community.

With a $25 per month recurring donation, you can sponsor one ride per month to a Hall County rider living on limited income.

Our goal is to collect 100 recurring donations of $25 per month each.

Please donate today to help a Hall County senior rider.
